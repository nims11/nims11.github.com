--- layout: post title: Ultimate Playlist June ---

New ritual! Every end of month, I will write about the stuffs I listened to and prepare an ultimate playlist for that month accordingly. Just a mean to keep track of my musical tastes.

Mastodon steals a lot of the show for this month. Having only tried Blood and Thunder, I gave the much hyped Leviathan album a chance and it blew my mind away. This album was on repeat for days. The harsh riffs and drums were pretty hard to keep away from my mind even when not listening to it. Mastodon's Once more round the sun came out couple of days ago, but will try out the older works before coming to this one.

An unlikely detour from the kind of music I usually listen to, Pink Floyd came back to me this month. It was the Division Bell this time on repeat for days. It is my favourite from the big pool of other great Floyd albums.

After big impact The Sound of Perseverence had on my musical tastes, I also gave a casual retry to the much applauded Death's Symbolic. Few songs caught my attention and the rest surely will in the coming month.

Within Temptation's Hydra had come out on start of this year, but I hadn't given it a chance. After a listen, wasn't impressed much. Let us Burn and Paradise (which also features Tarja) were the only ones which got some playtime from me for a week.

One of my favourite Indian metal bands, the prog-metal/djent Skyharbor released a single Evolution from their upcoming album. The quality of the recording is very impressive. Anup Sastry is brilliant on drums as usual, with Dan Tompkins' vocals fitting well with the atmosphere the song creates. Can't wait for the full album! :)

The month ended with the Prog Rock Fusion band from India, Paradigm Shift gaining a big fan. I had tried couple of their songs before and loved them. After listening to the whole album Coalescence, it was tough to believe, this band is still miles away from being popular. "What's wrong with you, Indian *music fans*?!". The album just cost Rs 80! so I had no reason for not buying this.

Playlist

  • Evolution (Skyharbor)
  • I am Ahab (Mastodon)
  • Empty Words (Death)
  • Let us Burn (Within Temptation)
  • Iron Tusk (Mastodon)
  • Hearts Alive (Mastodon)
  • Paradise (Within Temptation)
  • Marooned (Pink Floyd)
  • In Twilight's Grace (Kryptos)
  • Coalescence (Paradigm Shift)
  • Lost for Words (Pink Floyd)
  • Sapna (Paradigm Shift)
