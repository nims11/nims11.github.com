One of the best months in term of music for me. This shortest month of the year was filled with too much music.

I started off with my fourth **Death** album, *Human*. Although not at the level of *Spiritual Healing*, *Sound of Perseverence*, and *Symbolic*, it is a great album nevertheless. I also tried one half of the latest **Obituary** album, *Inked in Blood*. It is the kind of death metal I love; catchy and highly dense riffs.

Rest of the month was spent in awe of a band I never really paid much attention but was always there in my playlist. I was aware of its potential since few years and this month I finally let it unleash upon me, the true brilliance in the avatar of **Opeth**. Having only tried top songs till now, I gave the album *Still Life* a try. It blew me away from the first listening session. Behind a combination of well placed rough and clean vocals, backed up by an insane level of complex and beautiful guitar work, there lies a sea of emotions, into which Opeth manages to let you take a dip.

This month, I plan on listening to more Opeth, complete the *Inked in Blood* album, and try a little **Deftones**.

### Playlist
- Flattening of Emotions (Death)
- Suicide Machine (Death)
- Lack of Comprehension (Death)
- Cosmic Sea (Death)
- Inked in Blood (Obituary)
- Visions in My Head (Obituary)
- Violent By Nature (Obituary)
- The Moor (Opeth)
- Godhead's Lament (Opeth)
- Face of Melinda (Opeth)
- Moonlapse Vertigo (Opeth)
- Eula (Baroness)
