There was quite a less metal this month.
This month was a mix of **Arch Enemy**, **Gojira**, and **Kan Gau**. The album of the month is Arch Enemy's *Anthems of Rebellion*. Just like I mentioned last month, Arch Enemy makes music which is very melodeathy and I will definitely like it for some time. *Anthems of Rebellion* is a brilliant album which played a lot to begin my year. All the songs are equally good, including the annoyingly catchy disco death metal "Leader of The Rats" (I can seriously imagine people in disco dancing on this track).

No new **Death** or **Skyharbor** this month :( I tried and like couple of more **Gojira** tracks from *From Mars to Sirius*. Rest of the music this month was about full-of-feel-piano scores from an awesome game (sort of) I finished recently, called *To the Moon* (Will write about it on a separate post). **Kan Gau** uses trivial sounding melody to produce something beautiful. Also a mention to **Laura Shigihara** for her vocals in *Everything's Alright*. Despite the main reason for my love for this OST being how well it went with the game, who cares?!
