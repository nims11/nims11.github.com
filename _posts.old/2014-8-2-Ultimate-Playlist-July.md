Last month I said about __Death's__ [Symbolic](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zbp60IX_jFQ&list=PL95CCC7F79B84FB7A) being one of the next possible _album of the month_ for me, and hell was I any more right about it! I might as well put the whole Symbolic on this month's ultimate playlist.

_Symbolic_ blew me away, and in no time, I got hooked to it. My energy level skyrocketed while listening to it leading me to do all kind of things from frequent headbanging, to singing along the rough vocals and doing air drumming and guitar throughout. __Chuck Schuldiner__ was a genius, and is rightlyfully _The Father of Death Metal_. RIP Chuck \m/

There was nothing more significant this month. __Paradigm Shift__ fever continued through the beginning of this month with me spending more time on the heavier tracks. Some random tracks from Dark Tranquility, Cradle of Filth, Insomnium, and Floyd paved their way into my recent listening list.

Plans for August? Maybe more Death and Opeth works. Also thinking of trying Gojira, new Judas Priest album, ...

### Playlist
- Zero Tolerance (Death)
- 1,000 Eyes (Death)
- Misanthrope (Death)
- Perennial Quest (Death)
- Batlaado (Paradigm Shift)
- Down With The Sun (Insomnium)
- What Only You Know (Dark Tranquility)
- The Foetus Of A New Day Kickin (Cradle of Filth)
- Wearing the Inside Out (Pink Floyd)
- Tere Liye (Paradigm Shift)
